What is Air Pollution?



Air pollution in Yangon happens when harmful gases, smoke, and dust from vehicles, factories, and burning waste make the air dirty and unsafe to breathe. It can cause health problems like asthma and coughing, especially for children and older people, and also makes the city less clean and pleasant. Therefore, it is important to reduce pollution and protect the environment.

FAQ

What is air pollution?

Air pollution is when harmful gases, smoke, dust, and chemicals mix into the air and make it unsafe for humans, animals, and the environment.

What causes air pollution?

Air pollution is caused by vehicles, factories, burning trash, and other activities that release harmful substances into the atmosphere.

Why is air pollution dangerous?

Air pollution can cause breathing problems, lung diseases, and damage to the environment and climate.

How can we reduce air pollution?

We can reduce air pollution by planting trees, recycling, using public transportation, and avoiding burning waste.

How can students help the environment?

Students can help by spreading awareness, keeping areas clean, saving energy, and reducing plastic use.
